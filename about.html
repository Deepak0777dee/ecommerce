Our Story

We Are Stackly

Born from a passion for quality and a belief that everyone deserves access to the world's finest products.

The Beginning

From a Garage
to Global

Stackly started in 2020 with a simple idea: build the most curated, beautiful, and trustworthy place to shop for premium products. Our founders, frustrated by generic e-commerce experiences, decided to create something different.

What began as a small team of 4 in a garage in San Francisco has grown into a global team of over 200 passionate people, serving customers in 50+ countries. We've shipped over 1 million orders and counting.

Our promise remains the same: every product on Stackly is hand-vetted by our team. We only carry what we'd buy ourselves.

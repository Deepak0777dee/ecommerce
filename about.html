We believe everyone deserves access to the freshest, most nutritious produce — sourced directly from our partner farms and delivered straight to your door.

Our Mission

Connecting Farms to Families

Stackly Shop was founded in 2020 with a simple but powerful mission: eliminate the middleman and bring farm-fresh produce directly to your table. We partner exclusively with certified organic farms, ensuring that every piece of fruit you receive is picked at peak ripeness within 24–48 hours of delivery.

Our technology-driven supply chain maintains a precise cold-chain from farm to doorstep, preserving nutrients, flavor, and freshness in ways that conventional supermarkets simply cannot match.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make — from how we source our produce to how we treat our customers.

🌿

Sustainability

We work exclusively with farms that use sustainable, pesticide-free practices. Our packaging is 100% biodegradable.

🤝

Fair Partnerships

We pay our farm partners fair prices, building long-term relationships that support rural communities.

✨

Uncompromising Quality

Every product passes a 7-point freshness inspection before it's approved for delivery. No exceptions.

💚

Community Health

We believe access to fresh, nutritious food is a right, not a privilege. We're working to make it affordable for everyone.

🔬

Science-Backed Freshness

Our cold-chain technology and harvest timing are guided by food science research to maximize nutrient retention.

📦

Radical Transparency

We tell you exactly where each product comes from, how it was grown, and when it was harvested. No secrets.
